Permissions Required

Meetlify needs macOS recording permissions before it can capture system audio and microphone input.

How to enable access

  1. Open System Settings.
  2. Go to Privacy & Security.
  3. Open Screen Recording.
  4. If Meetlify is not listed yet, click the + button and add Meetlify.app from your Applications folder.
  5. Enable Meetlify in Screen Recording.
  6. Open Microphone and enable Meetlify there too if you want mic recording.
  7. Quit and reopen Meetlify after changing permissions.
